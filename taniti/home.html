Welcome to Taniti!

While the island has an area of less than 500 square miles, the terrain is varied and includes both sandy and rocky beaches, a small but safe harbor, lush tropical rainforests, and a mountainous interior that includes a small, active volcano. We invite all to sample Taniti's wonderful sights and unforgettable cuisine!
